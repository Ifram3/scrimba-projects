Visit Lagos

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant state, which serves as Nigeria's economic powerhouse and cultural hub.

Top five fun activities to do at Lagos

Experience a thearitical performance or cultural event

Terra Kulture is a cultural hub featuring Nigerian art, literature, and performance arts, along with a restaurant serving traditional cuisine

Enjoy a day of swimming, surfing, or relaxing by the water.

Tarkwa Bay beach is a serene and sheltered beach known for its tranquil waters, making it deal for swimming, surfing, and relaxation

Visit the zoo, enjoy rides, or splash in the water park.

Omu Resort is a family-friendly destination featuring a zoo, amusement park, water park, and diverse recreational activities.

Immerse yourself in the energetic nightlife and entertainment

Quilox is a high-energy nightclub known for its luxurious ambiance, top-notch DJs, and vibrant nightlife experience

Enjoy live jazz music while browsing books and sipping coffe

Jazz Hole is a cozy cultural hub where visitors can enjoy live jazz music whike exploring a curated selection of books, arts, and coffee

Historical Cultures/Landmarks

Nike Art Gallery

This is a renowned cultural institution showcasing a vast collection of Nigerian art, including tradtional and contemporary pieces.

Lekki Conservation Center

This is a sprawling nature reserve known for its canopy walkway and diverse wildlife, offering a serene escape into nature.

Freedom Park

This is a historic landmark turned cultural center, featuring art exhibtion, live music, and a serene enviroment
